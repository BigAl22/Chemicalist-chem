EARTH SMP

  1. Creation:

    • The map is created and launched at 12 noon on December 2nd 2019

  1. 4 Nations Created:

    • Babylon was created by Matt. Location: Egypt and India

    • Indonesia was created by Jonathan. Location: Indonesia

    • The New Soviet Union (NSU) was created by Alexei. Location: Russia

    • Morocco was created by Greg. Location: Morocco

  2. The Potato Pact is Signed:

    • In the first few minutes of the server’s birth, a baby zombie attacked Matt, the ruler of Babylon while he was severely undergeared and had no way to defend himself. Jon, the ruler of Indonesia, then bravely stepped in and killed the baby zombie saving Matt. When the zombie was slain, it dropped a potato, a very rare drop from a zombie. Expressing his gratitude, Matt decided to form an alliance with Jon, and calling it The Potato Pact, to remember how the baby zombie dropped a potato

  3. The Soviet-African Alliance is Created:

    • After the recent creation of The Potato Pact, Matt and Jon soon bragged about their alliance, saying that they were “The most powerful on the server”. Alexei, the ruler of The New Soviet Union, became nervous about the growing ego of Matt and Jon. he then turned to Greg, and asked to form an Alliance with him. This action made Matt nervous, as Russia and Africa both need items from each other and it could become an extremely beneficial alliance. Matt countered Alexei’s proposition and asked Greg if he would join their alliance, saying “All Alexei does is starve his people, and he’ll starve you like normal Africa”. Alexei then said if greg did not accept his proposition of an alliance, he would terminate greg’s website that he had made. Greg really liked the website, so he decided to ally with Alexei, forming the Soviet-African Alliance.

  4. The First Building is Created:

    • After arriving in India by using Jon’s Boat, Matt decided to create a Mining outpost (much later on, Matt named the outpost “Karr” with the help of Jon). It is currently still in use.

  5. The First Trade:

    • After Alexei and Jon had gotten to Russia and Indonesia respectively, they realized that they both needed stuff that was far away from their base camps. They decided to make a deal. Jon offered 17 Eggs for 2 birch, spruce, and jungle saplings plus an additional potato. Both of them decided to meet in Africa, but Jon was much farther away than he thought, so they decided to conduct the trade in the middle of the Indian Ocean.

  6. The First Diamond Mined:

    • When Mining in his Outpost in India, Matt stumbled upon an enormous cave that he named “Orpheus”. Inside, Matt found a vein of 2 diamonds. He named the first diamond he mined “The First Diamond” and he used the other diamond for a Shovel.

  7. The First "Railroad" is Constructed:

    • Wanting to get to Orpheus faster, Matt built a short underground railroad. It has yet to be ridden on.

  8. The Great Leap Foreward:

    • After spending sometime in Russia, Alexei realized that no animals spawn up in the freezing dense regions, so he needed to move up some animals from down south. After making a journey to africa, he found some cows and started to herd them back to russia. After many deaths and the aid of Matt and Jon was Instated, he finally got the cows back up to russia in around an hour.

  9. 1 Nation Created:

    • United States of Minecraft (USM) was created by Mark. Location: United States of America

  1. 2 Nations Created:

    • Germany was created by Brandon. Location Germany

    • Austria was created by Jimmy. Location Austria

  2. The Allied World Powers (AWP) Alliance is Created:

    • After Spending sometime together, Brandon and Jimmy thought that they should be allied together, since they are both “Incredibly stupid and dumb.” they called this alliance the “AWP”, standing for “Allied World Powers”. After a few hours, they invited Mark, the Ruler of the USM to join for the reason of “Keeping Brandon and Jimmy in check”.

  3. The End Portal is Found:

    • While exploring North America, Mark stumbled upon the endportal. Because of how important this is, Mark hasn’t revealed that much information on the topic.

  4. The First Enchantment Table is Crafted:

    • After scoring a lucky find in the caves, Brandon found a vein of 5 diamonds. He used the first 2 to create an enchantment table, and another three for a diamond pickaxe.

  5. The First Nether Portal is Made:

    • Using the diamond pickaxe, brandon mined 10 obsidian and built the first nether portal in germany. He then used this portal and became the first person to become to enter the nether.

  6. 3 Nations Created:

    • France was created by Katie. Location: France

    • Canada was created by Lauren. Location: Canada

    • China was created by Fake Brandon. Location: China

  7. The French-German Border Crisis:

    • After France had been founded, Katie had decided to settle on the western side of france, so west that it was actually in Germany’s borders. Though Brandon didn’t technically own all of the German land however, he did express anger and threatened to use force if Katie didn’t Move her building. The conflict resolved when Katie decided to move her house to the border of Spain and France.

  8. The Banana Alliance is Formed:

    • After Lauren and Fake Brandon settled down and started to colonize, Lauren suggested the idea of an alliance. This created The Banana Alliance.

  9. 1 Nation Created:

    • Norway was created by Emma. Location: Norway
